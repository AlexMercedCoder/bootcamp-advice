Guide for Those Considering a Coding Bootcamp

Part 1 - Setting Expectations before Day 1

Watch this video to set expectations going into a bootcamp. Whether doing a three or six month course you should dedicate as much time as possible to your career change and mentally be prepared to spend 1000 hours of prep in and out of your bootcamp before you start to see progress on your job search (about 750 will be used up in class and doing the homework from class).
